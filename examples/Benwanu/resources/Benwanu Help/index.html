Moving Around

Click and drag regions to zoom. Move around with the arrow keys. Zoom in and out with 'i' and 'o'.
Fit to the original image with 'f'.
Go back to prior selections with 'u'.

Parallelism

Use the Threads menu to choose the number of updating threads.

Extras

Use File->New to display more windows. Use File->Save on your favorite discoveries.
